Portrait Photography

Focuses on capturing the personality and mood of an individual or group of people.
This can include traditional portraits, candid shots,
or creative interpretations.

Architectural Photography

Specializes in photographing buildings and other man-made structures. It focuses on both the aesthetic and functional aspects of design.

Aerial photography involves capturing images of the Earth from an elevated position, usually from aircraft, drones, satellites, or balloons. It provides a bird's-eye view of landscapes, cities, and various terrains, allowing for a unique perspective that is impossible to achieve from the ground.
